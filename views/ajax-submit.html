Attributes
data-ajax-listen
A Context Selector pointing to the element(s) to listen for events on
data-ajax-target
A Context Selector pointing to the element(s) that ajax-submit events will be sent to
data-ajax-events
The events that will trigger an ajax-submit
Events
ajax-submit
Triggered by the events specified by the data-ajax-events attribute
Description

Responds to events specifed by the data-ajax-events attribute to trigger an ajax-submit event.

This behavior is used to trigger the sending of an ajax-form.

Example
HTML
<form data-behavior="ajax-form"
         action="http://my-service"
         method="get"
         target="#frame">

         <input type="search" name="query"
                  data-behavior="ajax-submit"
                  data-ajax-events="keyup" />
         <button type="submit">Send</button>
</form>

<div  data-behavior="ajax-frame"
         id="frame">
         Enter your search to see results...
</div>
